Rules

  • Each week players pick one team from the set of premier league fixtures
  • If the team wins the player goes through to the next round
  • If the team loses or draws the player is out
  • Players can only pick each team once during the competition
  • Players who are still alive must pick their team before the weeks deadline
  • Players who fail to pick a team will be assigned the next alphabetical team available to them
  • If a players team has their game postponed the player will qualify for the next round and the team cannot be picked again
  • If your chosen team playes twice in a gameweek only the first game will be counted
  • If all of the remaining players are eliminated all of these player will go through to the next round
  • The decision of the committe is always final
  • The Last one standing is the winner
